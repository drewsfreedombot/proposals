Discovery Summary

The Opportunities We Found

The key challenges in your current workflow and how we'll solve them.

The "10-to-6" Problem

Your setters work 10 AM to 6 PM, but your leads come in 24/7. Any lead generated outside business hours sits cold until the next day, killing the speed-to-lead advantage. Charlie works around the clock, engaging every lead within 60 seconds, ensuring you never lose momentum.

The 40% No-Show Rate

A 55-60% show rate is average, but it means 4 out of 10 qualified prospects are never spoken to. That's a massive leak in the funnel. Our 7-step show rate sequence, which impersonates the closer, is designed to significantly increase your show rate.

The "Maybe" Pipeline

Your setters call leads 6-10 times, but then what? As you said, it "doesn't always happen" consistently. This creates a huge pipeline of "maybes" — leads that weren't a hard "no" but were never fully worked. Charlie's core philosophy is to **follow up forever** with data-driven persistence until a yes or no is received, turning that "maybe" pipeline into revenue.

The Untapped Goldmine

You admitted to doing "very little" with old leads and having only a manual referral process. Your database of past prospects is a goldmine. Charlie can reactivate them with contextually relevant messages and automate referral requests from happy customers, creating two new, powerful lead channels.

Our human setters work Monday through Friday, like, 10 a.m. to 6. And then there's just so much that doesn't happen between those hours, that we know there's room for improvements.

Our Technology

Why Charlie is Different

You've evaluated other platforms. Here’s why they "still suck" and Charlie doesn't.

You mentioned other platforms feel like AI support agents you have to force into a sales role. Charlie was built from the ground up for one purpose: to be an elite sales development AI. This is powered by our "Four Judges" system, which ensures every single message is on-brand, intelligent, and effective.

The Four Judges: How Charlie Stays On-Brand & In Control

Before Charlie sends any message, it's reviewed by four specialized AIs that act as an internal quality control council. This prevents the "hallucinations" and off-brand comments common in other platforms.

  • Judge 1: Prospect Congruence — Mirrors the prospect’s tone and communication style to build rapport.
  • Judge 2: Your Feedback — Learns from every piece of feedback you provide. If you like a response, it does more of it. If you don't, it never makes that mistake again.
  • Judge 3: Conversational Stage — Understands where it is in the sales process. It knows when to build rapport, when to dig for pain, and when to ask for the meeting.
  • Judge 4: Your Instructions — Obeys your specific rules. If you tell Charlie "don't accept surface-level answers," it will politely re-ask questions until it gets the information it needs.

Human-Like Nuance

Simple AI bots break when faced with real human conversation. If a prospect says, "I'm at work, message me later," most bots fail. Charlie understands, sets a reminder, and re-engages at 5 PM. It's these "little shavings," as Drew said, that add up to a truly intelligent and effective conversational experience.

Native HubSpot Integration

Charlie works inside the CRM you already use. All conversations, notes, prospect goals, and hurdles are automatically synced to the deal card in HubSpot, giving your closers perfect context for every call.
