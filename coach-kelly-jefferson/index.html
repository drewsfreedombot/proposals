What We Heard

The key challenges and opportunities you shared with us

📉 750 Registrants Aren't Showing Up Every Week

You're pulling 1,000–1,200 leads per week into the top of your funnel, but only about 25% are showing up to the live workshop. That's roughly 750 people every single week who registered, raised their hand, and then didn't attend. Your setters are calling registrants to try to book pre-workshop calls, but the Sales Kick audit revealed they're dialing at half the capacity they should be. Charlie responds in under 60 seconds, 24/7, and follows up persistently with every registrant — acting as Kelly's assistant, building rapport, surfacing their goals, and making sure they show up on Tuesday. Getting this number from 250 to 400+ attendees per week — without spending another dollar on ads — is the single highest-leverage move available.

🔄 100,000+ Leads Sitting Untouched

You've accumulated over 100,000 leads in GoHighLevel that haven't been re-engaged. Your setters and closers are fully consumed by the weekly cycle — new leads come in, workshop runs Tuesday, closers work the rest of the week, and then it starts over. There's no bandwidth left to work the existing database. A single reactivation campaign targeting these leads — especially those where Charlie already captured goals and pain points during the initial conversation — is typically where we see the fastest and most significant ROI.

📊 60% Show Rate to Closer Calls — Room to Grow

When prospects book a call with your closers, 60% show up. Most of our clients with similar funnels see 70–80%. Charlie's show rate agent — texting as the closer from a separate number minutes after booking — adds the personal touch and urgency that pushes this number up. The closer hops on the call knowing the prospect is prepped, has reviewed the materials, and is ready to make a decision.

🚪 Post-Webinar Drop-Off

After the workshop, Kelly pitches the $5K program and directs attendees to an application. But not everyone fills out that application right away — and those who don't tend to go cold quickly. Charlie follows up with every attendee who didn't complete the application, surfaces what resonated with them on the workshop, and drives them back to the application or directly onto a call.

👥 Four Setters — Where Do They Fit?

Kelly asked the right question: "What does the setter role become?" The answer is phased. Phase 1 fills holes where your setters aren't spending time today (reactivation, webinar show rate, post-webinar follow-up). Phase 2 puts Charlie head-to-head with your setters on their existing workflow — and you look at the data. More often than not, Charlie performs stronger. Your setters can then shift to higher-value phone work, or you can reduce headcount over time as confidence builds.

Phase 1 — Weeks 1–2: Fill the Gaps

Build, Spar, and Launch Core Agents

  • Monday: Kickoff call (1 hour) — Build the first two agents together: webinar show rate agent and reactivation agent. Load knowledge base, set Kelly's persona and voice, define qualification criteria, connect GHL integrations and triggers.
  • Tuesday–Thursday: Sparring — Roleplay as prospects, fire objections, test edge cases. Give real-time feedback: positive (Charlie does more of it) and constructive (Charlie never does it again). Half-hour calls each day until Charlie is a 10/10.
  • Friday: Integration testing — Test GHL triggers, custom field pushes, calendar booking, and SMS delivery. Deploy when ready.
  • Week 2: Charlie is live on webinar registrants and reactivation database. Monitor conversations daily (10 minutes), provide feedback, optimize. Launch post-webinar follow-up agent once the first two are stable.
  • Your existing setters and closers continue unchanged — Charlie fills holes where they aren't today.
Phase 2 — Weeks 2–4: Optimize the Close

Show Rate + Written Closer Agents

  • Launch Closer Call Show Rate Agent — Texts from the closer's number, confirms prep, drives show rate from 60% toward 80%
  • Launch Post-Call Written Closer — Picks up warm leads that didn't close on the first call, follows up as the closer
  • Head-to-head testing (optional): Give each of your 4 setters 20% of leads and Charlie the remaining 20%. Compare the data. Adjust allocation based on performance.
  • Ongoing optimization: weekly conversation reviews, script refinement, A/B testing messaging approaches
Phase 3 — Month 2+: Expand & Retain

Referrals, NPS, and Scale

  • Launch Referral & NPS Agent — Automated outreach to past clients for feedback and affiliate referrals
  • Gradually increase Charlie's share of inbound volume as confidence builds
  • Additional agents available: cancellation rebooking, no-show recovery, payment link nudges, onboarding sequences

⚠️ One Rule

Once an agent is performing — don't touch it. The biggest issue we see is clients making changes to a working agent and cutting their numbers in half. Work with us to optimize; once it's dialed in, let it run. We'll tell you when something needs adjusting.
