The Opportunities We Found

The key leakage points in your current funnel and how we'll solve them.

The 46% Qualified Lead Gap

This is your single biggest opportunity. You've identified a perfect customer avatar, but **only 54% of them book a call**. This means nearly half of your most valuable, pre-qualified leads are slipping away. Charlie will engage this segment with a supportive, on-brand conversation to capture that lost revenue.

Monetizing the Other 60%

You disqualify 60% of your leads from high-ticket coaching, but they are still perfect candidates for your $197 DIY "Planning Suite." Charlie will act as a gentle guide for this segment, using gap selling to help them understand why the DIY offer is the right fit and ensuring a much higher conversion rate than a simple upsell page.

The Dialer Dilemma

Your experience with the dialer team is common: more calls, but lower quality, lower close rates, and no real profit lift, all while being emotionally taxing to manage. Your clients are in a sensitive state; they need a concierge, not a cold call. Charlie provides a supportive, brand-congruent experience that builds trust and converts better.

Maintaining Your High Show Rate

Your current 75% show rate is excellent because you have high-intent buyers. As we dramatically increase booking volume from less-ready leads, maintaining that rate becomes the next challenge. Our automated show-rate sequences are designed to solve this before it becomes a problem.

Your AI Team

What We're Building for You

A multi-agent deployment to plug every leak in your funnel.

🎯 The Qualified Lead Converter

This is your primary growth engine. The moment a lead qualifies via your application but doesn't book a call, this agent will engage them. It will act as a friendly, supportive concierge, using gap-selling questions to understand their specific hurdles and gently guide them to book a call, capturing the 46% of qualified leads you're currently missing.

💰 The DIY Upsell Agent

This agent focuses on the 60% of leads you disqualify from high-ticket coaching. Instead of just showing them an upsell page, this agent will start a conversation, acknowledge their situation, and explain why the $197 "Planning Suite" is the perfect next step for them. This conversational approach will dramatically increase your upsell conversion rate.

📈 The Show Rate Protector

To support the new influx of bookings, this agent will activate the moment a call is booked. Impersonating the closer, it will run a sequence to confirm the appointment, build rapport, and set expectations. This maintains your high 75% show rate even as call volume from less-qualified leads increases, ensuring your closers' time is always respected.

A Persona Built for Trust

Your clients are making life-altering decisions. The AI persona we design will be supportive, empathetic, and professional — a perfect extension of the Lemonade Life brand. This builds the trust necessary to move prospects to the next step in a way a generic dialer or simple automation never could.
