Discovery Summary

What We Heard

The key challenges and opportunities surfaced during discovery

⚡ The 46% Qualified Lead Gap

The single biggest opportunity. 82% of leads take the application, 45% qualify — but only 54% of those qualified leads actually book a call. That means 46% of the best, pre-qualified prospects are slipping away after Leanne's funnel already did the hard work of qualifying them. Charlie will engage this segment immediately with a supportive, brand-congruent conversation — using gap selling to surface their specific situation (what's at stake financially, emotionally, for their kids) and guide them to book.

💰 Monetizing the Disqualified 60%

60% of leads are disqualified from high-ticket coaching (DV situations, too far in process, undecided). But they're still perfect candidates for the $197 one-click "Planning Suite" upsell. Currently, they see a static upsell page. Charlie will start a conversation instead — acknowledge their situation, use gap selling to surface their hurdle ("what's been the main challenge?"), and when they articulate why they can't do it alone, guide them to the $197 DIY product. If they don't check out, Charlie follows up until they do.

📞 The Dialer Dilemma

The dialer team (via Dialer.io, Cole Gordon methodology) has strong speed to lead but is cannibalizing direct call bookings. Close rate has dropped significantly. More call volume but same ROAS/profit as before — "it just feels like so much work." Emotionally taxing to manage for this sensitive audience. Leanne was thinking of dropping the dialer entirely the morning of the call. Charlie provides a more congruent, text-based alternative that matches the sensitivity these women need.

📈 Protecting Show Rates at Scale

Current show rate is strong because leads are buyers with real pain — they see the strategy call as genuinely valuable ("we position it as a chance to dig into your biggest challenge and point out blind spots"). As Charlie increases booking volume from the 46% of missed qualified leads, maintaining that rate is critical. Show rate optimization agents activate immediately post-booking — impersonating the closer, confirming the appointment, and setting expectations with social proof and scarcity.

What We're Building for You

Three starter agents targeting the highest-impact leakage points — with room to expand

Step 1 (Day 0–2) — Close the Gap

These agents target the two biggest leakage points — the 46% of qualified leads who don't book and the 60% who get disqualified but aren't being monetized. Dialer team continues running in parallel. No disruption to current operations.

🎯 Qualified Lead Converter (Application Submitted → Book Call)

The primary growth engine. When a lead qualifies via the application but doesn't book a call, Charlie reaches out within 60 seconds. Acts as a friendly, supportive concierge — not a dialer, not a hard sell. Uses gap selling: "Hey, saw you filled out the application — curious, what are you hoping to accomplish through this? What does success look like?" When they share the hurdle (custody fears, financial complexity, feeling overwhelmed), Charlie guides them to book. Most sets happen within 5 minutes of surfacing the hurdle — because that's when they're writing down why they can't do this alone. Follows up persistently until they book or tell us to stop.

💰 DIY Upsell Agent (Disqualified → $197 Planning Suite)

Targets the 60% disqualified from coaching. Instead of a static upsell page, Charlie starts a conversation: "Hey, saw you filled out the application but didn't get the ticket — I was hoping to ask a couple questions to help you further." Surfaces their hurdle through gap selling. When they articulate why they need help, Charlie says: "Honestly, I'd encourage you to get the Planning Suite — there's a lot in there. 📈 Show Rate Protector (Booked → Show Up Prepped)

Five minutes after a booking, Charlie reaches out impersonating the closer: "Hey, it's [closer name] here — saw your appointment come through, looking forward to our call. Social proof + scarcity + warmth. If there's pre-call material (video, one-pager), Charlie sends it and confirms the prospect has reviewed it. Between the confirmation and prep check, this drives a significant lift in show rate as booking volume scales up.
