60% of leads are disqualified from high-ticket coaching, but they're still perfect candidates for the $197 DIY "Planning Suite." Currently, these leads see an upsell page and most bounce. Charlie will start a conversation with this segment — acknowledge their situation, use gap selling to help them understand why the DIY offer is the right fit, and drive a much higher conversion rate than a static page.

The dialer team experiment is producing more calls but lower quality, lower close rates, and no real profit lift — all while being emotionally taxing to manage. Leanne's clients are in a sensitive, vulnerable state. They need a concierge, not a cold call. 📈 Protecting Show Rates at Scale

Leanne's current show rate is strong because her buyers are high-intent. As Charlie dramatically increases booking volume from the 46% of currently-missed qualified leads, maintaining that rate becomes the next challenge. Phase 1 — Week 1: Close the Gap

These agents target the two biggest leakage points in the funnel — the 46% of qualified leads who don't book and the 60% who get disqualified but aren't being monetized effectively.

🎯 Qualified Lead Converter

The primary growth engine. The moment a lead qualifies via the application but doesn't book a call, this agent engages them. Acts as a friendly, supportive concierge — uses gap-selling questions to understand their specific hurdles (custody fears, asset concerns, timeline pressure) and gently guides them to book. Captures the 46% of qualified leads currently being lost. Tone is warm, empathetic, non-judgmental — a trusted advisor helping them take the next step.

💰 DIY Upsell Agent

Focuses on the 60% of leads disqualified from high-ticket coaching. Instead of just showing them an upsell page, this agent starts a conversation — acknowledges their situation, explains why the $197 "Planning Suite" is the perfect next step for where they are right now, and handles objections conversationally. This approach will dramatically increase upsell conversion vs. a static page. Same empathetic, supportive tone — these women still need help, just in a different format.

📈 Show Rate Protector

Activates the moment a call is booked. Impersonates the closer — confirms the appointment, builds rapport, sets expectations, and ensures the prospect is prepped and committed. As booking volume increases from the Qualified Lead Converter, this agent ensures show rates stay high. Uses a direct but warm approach: "Looking forward to our call — if you need to reschedule, let me know now so I can hold the spot."

Phase 2 — Weeks 2–3: Recovery & Scale

Once Phase 1 agents are performing well, we add recovery agents and consider replacing the dialer team entirely.

🚫 Cancellation Rebooking Agent

When a prospect cancels their strategy call, Charlie reaches out immediately to understand why and offer a convenient alternative. Warm, empathetic approach — acknowledges their situation (divorce is stressful, life gets in the way), removes friction, and gets them rescheduled before they go cold and lose momentum.

Cancellation Detection Empathetic Re-engagement Barrier Removal Rescheduling

📞 No-Show Rebooking Agent

When someone doesn't show up, Charlie follows up within minutes with a friendly, non-judgmental check-in. No guilt — just a simple "life happens, we understand" message with an easy path to rebook. Critical for this audience: women in divorce proceedings often have unpredictable schedules. A gentle recovery touch can save appointments that would otherwise be lost entirely.

  • Sensitivity: Divorce is deeply personal. Charlie must never be pushy, dismissive, or transactional. Phrases like "I totally understand" and "there's no rush" are essential.
  • Congruency goal: Charlie's language should match Leanne's brand voice — the same energy that drew these women in initially should carry through every touchpoint
