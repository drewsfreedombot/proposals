🎨 Brand Voice & Persona

  • Persona: Charlie shows up as Robby's assistant — warm, knowledgeable, genuinely interested in each person's health journey. Mirrors the Mastering Diabetes brand voice.
  • Custom training: We'll upload videos of Robby talking on Instagram/Facebook to build a persona that sounds like him — his energy, his way of explaining things, his enthusiasm.
  • Key behavior: Charlie should redirect methodology questions to the webinar or call — "Great question, that's exactly what Robby covers in the live training." Keep them curious, not over-educated in the DM.
  • Tone calibration: Professional but approachable. This demographic is dealing with a serious health condition — empathetic without being clinical. Think "knowledgeable friend" not "doctor's office."

⚙️ Technical Integration

  • CRM: GoHighLevel — deal stages updated automatically. Charlie moves qualified leads to "Booked," webinar registrants through the appropriate pipeline.
  • Booking: Calendly (natively integrated for conversational booking). iClosed integration is in development — for now, iClosed bookings would use a calendar link. Recommend starting with Calendly for the conversational advantage.
  • Instagram & Facebook DM: Charlie handles all comment-triggered DMs natively. Replaces ManyChat for keyword comments.
  • ManyChat: Keep for new follower outbound ONLY. ManyChat triggers the intro, Charlie takes over the conversation from there.
  • SMS: Currently on GHL native. We need to investigate the A2P compliance warnings before scaling. May recommend Twilio integration depending on findings.
  • LINQ (optional): iMessage-quality outreach at scale — $500/month + $250/line. 50 new leads/day per line with blue bubbles and read receipts. A2P built in. Not required to start, but worth considering to replace the personal iPhone workflow.

🧠 Knowledge Base (What We'll Feed Charlie)

  • masteringdiabetes.org — program details, method overview, testimonials, credibility markers
  • Robby's Instagram/Facebook videos — for persona building and voice matching
  • Webinar registration page and content — so Charlie can intelligently discuss what's covered
  • Key testimonials — real reversal stories Charlie can reference naturally ("We just had someone drop their A1C from 12.9 to 5.9")
  • Common objections — "I can't eat carbs with diabetes," "I've tried everything," "How is this different from keto?"
  • Book details — for low-ticket downsell conversations
  • Qualification criteria — who is a good fit for coaching vs. book-only vs. not ready yet

📊 Data Flow & Custom Fields

  • Goal capture: Charlie surfaces what the prospect wants ("drop my A1C below 6," "lose 30 pounds," "get off medication") → pushed to GHL custom field
  • Hurdle capture: What's been stopping them ("I've tried low-carb and it's not working," "my doctor says I need insulin") → separate field
  • Why this matters: Every downstream agent — show rate, post-call follow-up, reactivation — references these fields for personalized messaging
  • Webinar tracking: Registered, attended, didn't attend, attended-didn't-book — Charlie knows which stage each lead is in
